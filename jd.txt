About The Role.



We are seeking an experienced Senior Analytics Engineer to join our growing Data function at a pivotal stage of platform maturity.



This role is central to shaping the next phase of our analytics ecosystem - designing scalable, high-quality decision-making data models, strengthening governance and reliability, and enabling insight-driven decision-making across Product, Risk, Finance, and Commercial teams.

You will operate at the intersection of modelling architecture, workflow reliability, and technical standards, working closely with Data Analysts, Data Engineering, Data Science, Software Engineering, and senior business stakeholders.



This is a hands-on senior individual contributor role with meaningful influence over our data architecture and engineering culture.



If you are excited by building clean, scalable analytics systems in a fast-moving fintech environment - and want real ownership rather than narrow ticket execution - this role is for you.



Day-to-day responsibilities.



Domain Ownership & Analytics Modelling



Own modelling architecture for one or more business domains, ensuring scalability, clarity, and long-term maintainability
Design, build, and evolve high-quality dbt models and analytics-ready data marts
Translate complex business and product requirements into robust, well-documented transformation logic
Establish and enforce modelling standards, naming conventions, and domain boundaries
Drive Snowflake performance optimisation and cost governance initiatives
Lead structured refactoring initiatives to reduce technical debt and improve maintainability


Workflow, Orchestration & Reliability



Design and maintain Airflow DAGs and alerting frameworks (Dag Factory) to ensure reliable data workflows
Own operational reliability for analytics pipelines, including incident response and SLA adherence
Improve orchestration patterns, documentation standards, and monitoring practices
Collaborate with Data Engineering to maintain clear separation between infrastructure ownership and analytics layer responsibilities


Governance, Security & CI/CD



Contribute to secure data access design and role-based access control (RBAC) frameworks
Improve and maintain CI/CD processes for analytics workflows, ensuring safe and automated deployments
Implement automated linting, testing, and code review standards to elevate engineering quality
Strengthen lineage, documentation, and semantic governance across dbt and BI tooling
Partner with stakeholders to ensure data definitions are consistent, trusted, and audit-ready


Technical Leadership & Engineering Culture



Provide senior technical leadership through structured code reviews, peer learning sessions, and documentation practices
Mentor Analytics Engineers and Analysts transitioning into engineering-focused roles
Act as a modelling and data architecture subject matter expert within the organisation
Champion best practices in SQL, dbt, version control, and scalable analytics design


Cross-Functional Systems Design



Partner on strategic initiatives across Product, Risk, and Finance analytics infrastructure
Ensure analytics considerations are embedded early in architectural decisions
Influence how new systems integrate into the broader data platform
